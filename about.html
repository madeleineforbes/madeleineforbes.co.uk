I help individuals and small businesses connect with their clients and develop human-friendly systems, to make work feel peaceful.

I'm a writer, a noticer of the details. I studied English Language and Literature at Oxford University, and went on to work in Education, Local Government and Higher Education. I'm a trained proofreader, a self-taught coder and a natural problem-solver. Turning freelance in 2014 felt like coming home.

I'm learning Portuguese. I have been known to take naps during parties. As a child, I was an insatiable reader and teller of stories, and not a lot has changed.

I like all the good things. Tea, puns, yoga. I ride a bicycle and knit badly. I cook and walk and write things down every single day.
