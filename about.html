This is me eating a delicious plate of street food on a trip to my best friend's wedding in India, June 2014. Eating and travelling are two of my favourite things to do.

I'm a writer, a noticer of the details. I studied English at Oxford, before returning to London to work in special educational needs for a Local Authority. In 2011 I escaped with my partner, Nick, to travel by bicycle through Spain, Portugal and Morocco. Along the way I realised that working in an office was not how I wanted to spend the rest of my life and since then I've grown a sustainable business all of my own.

I'm an introvert, a trained proofreader, a self-taught coder and a natural problem-solver. Turning freelance in 2014 felt like coming home.

I like all the good things: Tea, puns, yoga. I sing and knit badly and dance enthusiastically. I cook and walk and write things down every single day. I have been known to take naps during parties. As a child, I was an insatiable reader and teller of stories, and not a lot has changed.
