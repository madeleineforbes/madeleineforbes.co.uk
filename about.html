Nice to meet you.

Being naturally anxious, I've taught myself to be brave so I can live the life I want to. I grew up in London, studied at Oxford, and now I live in a little house at the end of a dirt track in the mountains of central Portugal.

I'm a garrulous introvert, a trained proofreader, a self-taught coder and a terrible liar. I'm married to Nick; we share our home with two ill-behaved cats and a dog that invited itself in and never left.

Meditation keeps me sane-ish. I cook and walk and write every single day. As a child, I was an insatiable reader and teller of stories, and not a lot has changed.
