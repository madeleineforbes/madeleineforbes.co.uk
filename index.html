About

I'm layering the code on this website, slowly, as I make the transition from being a consumer of the web, to a builder of it.

It's a journey I'm interested in making in other areas, too. From buying food to growing it. From reading books to writing them.

I'm not a brave person, or a genius, but I know that if you want something you have to work for it. Change doesn't happen overnight. It's making a committment and sticking to it, tenaciously, and trusting that with time and effort I will gain new skills.

I write daily and have done since I was a child. I've never found anything I like to do more. I publish here from time to time, but any writer will tell you that what you leave out is as important as what you keep in. I delete, I start over, things change.

It's no use writing things if you're not building as well. Coders, you can look me up on github and see how sloppy this site is, how I'm feeling my way as I learn.

I no longer allow perfectionism to get in the way of starting things.

Fuel

I know as little as anyone does about the world, seeing only through these two eyes. I read as much as I can. The knowledge I absorb is fuel for my writing, and for learning how to live.
