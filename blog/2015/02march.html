Do you trust what you write?

If you're trying to write copy for your website, chances re you've spent time looking around at what other people are doing.

It's inevitable that some of that seeps into what you're writing.

How do you talk about your work?

How would you describe itto your mum? A brand new client? An exisiting client?

1. Share early, share often

Try taking a media holiday

2. Be OK with being a beginner

Try reading somerhing different. Try watching a film or drawing.

Read it aloud. Always.

Give yourself time

Read it to someone you know (my husband is good for this)

Best of all, leave it a few days and go back to it.
