Stationery

Saturday 22nd March, 2014

One of the main things people said to me when I told them I was quitting my desk job to go self-employed was “Make the most of the stationery cupboard!”


Across ages and genders, one of the main things fellow freelancers lamented, and office-bound colleagues enjoyed, was the palace of post-its, the shrine of paperclips and ring binders and giant balls of rubber bands.
