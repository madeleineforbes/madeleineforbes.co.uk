Writing can be hard


You start out with great intentions. The words flow; the ideas bubble out.

Then you read it back. It's brilliant! You're a genius.

You read it again. Ugh. Maybe not.

This time the words seem fake, false. You imagine someone reading it and sniggering. Are you trying too hard? Maybe it's better to keep it simple. Take out all the flowery stuff.

Now your site reads like an operator's manual. It's dry as a bone. Even YOU'RE bored by this.

If only there was someone who could tell you if you're on the right track.

See clearly


Imagine having the univided attention of an experienced, objective writer. Someone's who's also in tune with their intuition, who's not going to tell you off for writing how you talk or swearing or veering slightly off course.

Someone to find the thread in what looks like chaos. To tell you "Yeah, you've got it!" To be honest when their attention wavers, because that's when your clients' attention will wobble, too.

Someone honest. And skilled. And kind.
