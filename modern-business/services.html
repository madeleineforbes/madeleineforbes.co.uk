How may I help you?

  1. Home
  2. Services

I believe that work should feel peaceful.


I work with people who, like me, are creative, caring and curious about how they can use their gifts to make the world a better place. I tend to resonate with small, heart-led businesses and self-employed people whose work is based around connection, authenticity and communication.

It takes guts and soul to head out on your own. But walking your own path shouldn't mean spending your time hunched over a computer screen or going to bed with your phone instead of your lover.

Expressing your vision doesn't have to include starting your day with a slog through your inbox, or missing vital client communications because you're wrestling with a recalcitrant YouTube upload.

Maybe you're someone who's visually creative, but struggle to put your finger on the right words to express what you offer to those who need it.

Or you know you're incandescent in-person, but your heart and soul feel like they get somehow watered down in blogs or email to your clients. (That's if the tear-your-hair-out frustration of getting the words formatted and published don't stop your heartfelt words ever seeing the light of day in the first place)

Perhaps you're great at all of these things, but your love of the details is stealing your precious time away from growing and developing your business. Here's how I can help you.
