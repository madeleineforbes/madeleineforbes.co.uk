Is This You?

  1. Home
  2. Seeking Dreamers

Do you have a gift or a talent that you share with the world, but find difficult to put into words?

Are you an artist, a healer, or a creator of beautiful objects or experiences?

If you love what you do but find that when you come to write about it all the sparkle disappears, I want to get to know you better.

When you're connected to your flow, when you notice magic where others see only the everyday, it changes things for those around you. Reality shifts and possibilities open up where there were none.

But how do you write about what you do - on a website, in an email, in a tweet?

My passion is finding the perfect words to describe the indescribable. Yes, I'm partial to a paradox. I'm curious as to whether the wordsmithery I love could be of service. So if you're a creative person who dries up when they look at the page I want to get to know you.

I'm carrying out short (30 minute) research interviews with creative people who struggle with writing about their work.

We'll have a Skype chat, and to say thank you for your time I'll write you a short bio, for you to use wherever you want.

Want to be a part of this? How wonderful. Drop me an email at madeleine@madeleineforbes.co.uk with "research" in the title and I'll be in touch to fix a slot.

This is going to be fun :)
